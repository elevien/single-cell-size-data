Per-Cell Definitions

For cell cycle \(i\):

\[ \phi_i = y_{f,i} - y_{0,i} \]

where \(y = \log(\mathrm{size}/\langle \mathrm{size} \rangle)\).

\[ \tau_i = t_{f,i} - t_{0,i} \]

\[ \lambda_i = \frac{\phi_i}{\tau_i} \]

Columns in coarse_grained_parameters.csv

  • phi_mean: \(\langle \phi \rangle\), mean log-size increment per cycle.
  • phi_std: standard deviation of \(\phi\).
  • tau_mean: \(\langle \tau \rangle\), mean cycle duration.
  • tau_std: standard deviation of \(\tau\).
  • alpha: lineage correlation/statistical coupling parameter for size-control dynamics (computed from successive generations).
  • alpha_lambda: lineage correlation/statistical coupling parameter involving growth-rate fluctuations.
